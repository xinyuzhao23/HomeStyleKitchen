Lamb Chops


******

created by Diane Z.

Meat
Usually when people think of lamb chops, they think of dinner parties and special occasions. Of course they're perfect for dinner parties, but they can be made for a simple dinner to. Simply pop them in the oven and make some vegetable dishes, such as broccoli or green beans and you're good to go!
Ingredients:


  • 15-20 oz rack of lamb
  • Salt
  • Cumin

Cooking:


  1. Preheat the oven to 400 F.
  2. Cut the rack into chops by slicing it between the ribs. Make sure to cut it evenly so it will cook evenly.
  3. Cover a baking tray with aluminum foil and spray the entire cover with oil. Place the chops on the tray and put it in the oven once preheating is finished.
  4. Let the chops sit for 15 minutes for the full wrack and 10 minutes for half. Then flip each chop over and repeat the step.
  5. Place the chops on a plate and while it they are cooling, shake some salt and cumin onto one side of the chops. Flip and repeat. For the salt, using a salt grinder is preferable, but it is optional.
  6. Serve immediatly.
