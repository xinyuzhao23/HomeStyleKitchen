New York Steak


******

created by Diane Z.

Meat
New York Steak isn't just for parties and get-togethers. My family and I eat them for family lunches and dinners or whenever we want. You don't even have to have a fancy grill to make it. A pan (preferably non-stick or heavy metal) and oil is all you need. Just be careful not to burn it.
Ingredients:


  • 4 pack 16oz New York Steak
  • 24 teaspoons of salt (and extra for serving)
  • 24 teaspoons of pepper (and extra for serving)

Cooking:


  1. Place each steak on the a tray or a plate for seasoning. Then on each side of each steak, sprinkle 3 teaspoons of salt and 3 teaspoons of pepper. Using a salt grinder and pepper grinder is preferable, but not necessary.
  2. Let the steak sit for about 20 minutes to let it soak in the flavor.
  3. Coat the pan with oil.
  4. Once the oil is heated, put in the steak on one of its narrow sides. Cook for 5 minutes on high heat. Flip onto other narrow side and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Flip onto broad side. If you want well done, cook for 10 minutes. If you want medium to medium-well, cook for 8 minutes. Flip it over and repeat.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for the rest of the steaks (or as many steaks as you'd like).
  7. Serve immediatly. It is suggested to have some salt and pepper mixture on the side of the plate for flavor.
