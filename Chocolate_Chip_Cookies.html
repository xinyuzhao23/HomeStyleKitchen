Chocolate Chip Cookies


****

created by Xinyu Z.

Dessert
These aren't normal chocolate chip cookies. They're made from almond flour and the special flour gives these cookies a unique taste. It also causes the cookies not to flatten as much as normal chocolate chip cookies, but the interesting shape is what sets the cookies apart. Cookies made without any chocolate chips using this kind of flour tastes great as well. Just add in half the amount chocolate chips listed after you finish making the ordinary cookies. As a disclaimer, this is an altered version of a recipe I found on the back of an almond flour package to make the recipe dairy free.
Ingredients:


  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 3/4 cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Cooking:


  1. Using either an electronic mixer or a whisk, beat the sugar and the coconut oil in a large bowl until it is smooth.
  2. Beat in the egg until well blended.
  3. Mix the flour, baking soda, and salt in another large bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Slowly add the flour mixture into the wet ingredient mixture and mix thoroughly until it is blended together into dough.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  6. Carefully fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop up a tablespoon of dough and form a ball. Then flatten it with your hand until it's about 1.5 - 2 inches wide. Place on a nonstick baking stray, the bottom coated with vegetable oil, 2 inches apart from other cookies.
  8. Put the trays in the oven. Bake the top rack for 12-13 minutes and bake the middle rack for 20-22 minutes.
  9. Once finished baking, set aside to cool. Enjoy!
